ABOUT US

High 5 was founded by Holden, Jut King, Paradox and Dastrix in 2007. These members recruited Qedi into the team, creating a successful line-up. In the very early stages of South African eSports, competing was entirely for fun. No prize money and next to zero viewership. Our founders were of the few individuals in South Africa to grow and develop our competitive environment. Under the leadership of Holden and Dastrix, High 5 became one of the most respected competitive clans in South Africa. Historically, a player would enter trials to join various teams. High 5 differentiated themselves from this method, creating a prestige to joining - we would carefully handpick our players. In pursuit of his personal goals, Holden bid a sad farewell to the team to organize gaming tournaments and promote gaming as a whole for the South African community. It was at this time that Dastrix approached GiJoeSenior, Drew and Era to become owners of the team. The day-to-day running of the organisation was left to Drew and Era, with Dastrix and GiJoeSenior providing guidance where necessary. We pride ourselves on creating a family within our organisation. We place emphasis on our players, and their needs, above all else.
